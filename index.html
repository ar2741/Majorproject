Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI-powered healthcare assistant, online consultations, and insurance guidance.

How can I book a doctor’s appointment using the AI assistant?

Simply start a conversation with our chatbot and mention the kind of specialist you need. The assistant will show you available doctors and help you schedule your appointment online in just a few clicks.

Can the chatbot provide medical advice?

Our AI assistant can provide general health guidance, suggest possible conditions based on symptoms, and recommend next steps. However, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Is my personal and medical data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and follow strict data security standards. Your personal health information is encrypted and handled in compliance with healthcare data regulations.

Does the chatbot help with mediclaim and insurance queries?

Absolutely. The assistant can guide you on how to file a mediclaim, explain insurance policies, and connect you with your insurance provider’s support if needed.

Can I upload my health records or prescriptions?

Yes, you can securely upload your documents during the consultation or for future reference. The assistant will store them in your profile for easy access during follow-ups.

What happens if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

The chatbot can help you manage your appointments at any time. Just let it know, and it will walk you through cancellation or rescheduling without any hassle.
